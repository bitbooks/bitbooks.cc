Bitbooks turns a repo full of markdown files into a fully fledged, hosted, online book. For free.

What kind of books?

Creative-commons books for developer communities, like Scott Chacon's Pro Git, or Mark Pilgram's Dive into HTML5. Those guys are generous rockstars. Our mission is to pave the way for even more rockstars like them. Rockstars, like you.

If you can use Github, you can use Bitbooks.

From edits to pull requests, you manage all your content on Github, just the way you're used to. Commits to your public repo will automagically update your book behind the scenes.

Open source or bust.

We're sharing our code (starting with this page) and whatever we learn along the way.
